OUR FOUNDER


Nicole Yap, Founder and Editor-in-Chief of 8SIAN Magazine, was honored as one of Tatler's Asia's Most Influential in the technology industry in 2022. Following a successful launch and built a multi-million dollar brand and collaborated with billion-dollar companies such as Uniqlo, Vogue, Shop Cider, Royal Selangor, Charles & Keith, and more.
